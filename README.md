# DUET Medical Center Management System

A modern web-based Medical Center Management System designed for managing healthcare services, patient information, appointments, and medical operations efficiently.

This project was developed for the medical center management workflow of Dhaka University of Engineering & Technology (DUET), Gazipur.

---

# Project Overview

The DUET Medical Center Management System helps automate and simplify medical center activities including:

- Patient management
- Doctor management
- Appointment handling
- Medical records
- Healthcare administration
- Prescription and report management

The system aims to reduce manual paperwork and improve healthcare service efficiency through digital management.
